Ian Nater
Bio-

Hello! My name is Ian Nater and I was born in San Francisco California. I moved to Austin when I was three years old and have been living here since. I have a younger sister who works at Facebook in Austin as well as a younger brother who is a senior at UT. Hook em :). After High School I went to Southwestern University for a year before dropping out to help start a company called Briggo Coffee. Briggo is the worlds first fully robotic coffee barista hand made right here in Austin, Texas! I was fortunate enough to take the concept quite literally from a garage and scale it to multiple states and cities across the U.S. I even ended up in Dubai for a month installing one for the Crown Prince of Dubai. The business was recently sold and I am now taking time off while exploring what to do next. While at Briggo I was able to interact with our dev team daily and this sparked an interest in web dev for me. I am excited to be taking this course and learning all these awesome skills!
